What Causes Food Insecurity in the Philippines?

I N T R O D U C T I O N

Hello! The person who coded and wrote this entire web page is none other then me, Annikka S. Guevarra. I am a student of Sir JM from 9-Samat, he is currently teaching us the basics of making a web page using CSS and HTML. This web page is my first quarter project for both CS 3 and English 3. The content is purely satire, and is not meant to be taken seriously. I only intended to inform the public how immense the enormity of food insecurity is here in the Philippines.

M O T I V A T I O N   B E H I N D  T H E  W O R K

Other than being motivated because this is a major project, I wanted to learn more about food insecurity and learn ways on how I can assist my fellow FIlipinos. I believe this issue is not getting the attention it deserves, it is taking hundreds of lives all over the globe and yet we blame their death on themselves for their "laziness" or "ignorance." With this web page, I hope to inform some people the depravity and misery most people are enduring right now. Every hour, every minute, ever second, a person could die of inadequate access of food.
